Welcome Aspirant

Join a disciplined community focused on consistency, answer writing, peer learning and UPSC preparation.

Instructions & Declaration

Please read all instructions carefully before proceeding.

1. Consistency Commitment

You must maintain consistency in learning and regularly update your daily targets along with what you have learned throughout the day.

2. Daily Target Compliance

You are required to follow the daily targets provided in the group to maintain transparency and accountability.

3. Daily Answer Writing Commitment

Daily Mains questions will be provided in the group. You must write and submit your answers in the group before the end of the day.

4. Weekly Test Commitment

A practice test will be conducted every Sunday covering the topics studied from Monday to Saturday. Participation is mandatory.

5. Active Participation & Peer Learning

You must remain active in group discussions, help solve doubts and queries of fellow aspirants, and provide constructive suggestions on their answer writing whenever possible.

6. Inactivity & Removal Policy

Failure to maintain consistency, prolonged inactivity, or remaining merely a silent observer may lead to removal from the initiative with or without prior warning.

7. Prior Intimation for Absence

If you are unable to study, update targets, participate in discussions, or remain active due to any genuine reason, you are expected to inform the group in advance and mention the valid reason for your absence.

Final Declaration

I have carefully read and understood all the instructions mentioned above. I voluntarily agree to follow the rules, maintain discipline and consistency, actively contribute to the learning environment, and uphold the objectives of PrepCSE.

I understand that failure to comply with these guidelines may result in my removal from the initiative.
